Leche Flan

Ingredients

  • 1 can (12 oz) Evaporated milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) Sweetened condensed milk
  • 6 large Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup Granulated sugar (for caramel)

Instructions

  1. Make the caramel: In a small pan, melt the granulated sugar over low heat, stirring constantly, until it turns golden brown and becomes liquid. Carefully pour the hot caramel into a round baking pan (around 8-inch) to coat the bottom. Set aside to cool and harden.
  2. Prepare the flan mixture: In a large bowl, whisk together the evaporated milk, condensed milk, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Strain the mixture: To ensure a smooth texture, strain the flan mixture into the caramel-coated baking pan.
  4. Steam the flan: Place the pan in a steamer and cover it with a lid. Steam for about 30-40 minutes or until the flan is set (you can check by inserting a toothpick; it should come out clean).
  5. Cool and chill: Let the leche flan cool down to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours to fully set.
  6. Serve: Once chilled, run a knife along the edges of the flan and invert onto a plate to remove it from the pan. Slice and serve.
