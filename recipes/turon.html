Turon

Ingredients

  • 4 Saba Bananas
  • 1 cup Langka
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 10 Spring rolls
  • Cooking Oil

Instructions

  1. Prepare the bananas: Peel the saba bananas and slice them lengthwise into halves or quarters.
  2. Optional langka: If using, slice the langka into thin strips.
  3. Coat with sugar: Roll each piece of banana (and langka, if using) in sugar to coat it evenly.
  4. Wrap: Place the coated banana (and langka) on a spring roll wrapper, fold the sides, and roll it up tightly, sealing the ends with a little water.
  5. Fry: Heat oil in a pan and fry the turon until golden brown and crispy, about 3-5 minutes.
