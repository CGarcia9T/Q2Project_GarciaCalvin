Kare-kare

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Beef shank (or oxtail, cut into pieces)
  • 1/2 lb Tripas (beef tripe) (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Peanut butter (smooth)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Fish sauce (patis)
  • 1-2 tablespoons Annatto powder (for color, optional)
  • 4 cups Water
  • 2-3 medium Eggplants, sliced
  • 1 bunch String beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 Banana flower (or heart of banana), sliced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the beef: In a large pot, boil the beef shank and tripe (if using) in water for 45 minutes to 1 hour or until tender. Skim off the scum from the top.
  2. Sauté aromatics: In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil and sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Combine: Add the sautéed garlic and onion to the pot with the beef and add the fish sauce. Stir in the peanut butter and annatto powder. Add more water if needed to achieve a thick but smooth consistency.
  4. Add vegetables: Add the eggplant, string beans, and banana flower (if using). Simmer for about 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice and a side of bagoong (fermented shrimp paste).
