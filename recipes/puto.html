Puto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Rice flour
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1/4 cup Butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Cheese slices (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the batter: In a bowl, combine rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, water, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  2. Mix: Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
  3. Prepare the molds: Grease small steaming molds or silicone cups. Pour the batter into the molds about 2/3 full.
  4. Steam: Steam the puto over simmering water for about 20-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted.
  5. Top with cheese (optional): After steaming, top with a slice of cheese and steam for an additional 2-3 minutes until the cheese melts.
