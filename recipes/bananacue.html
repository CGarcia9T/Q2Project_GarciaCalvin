Banana Cue

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces saba bananas (ripe but firm, peeled)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • Bamboo skewers (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the oil: Heat the cooking oil in a deep frying pan or wok over medium heat. Make sure the oil is hot enough for frying.
  2. Coat the bananas: Once the oil is hot, carefully add the saba bananas into the pan. Fry for 1–2 minutes until the surface begins to soften.
  3. Caramelize the sugar: Sprinkle the brown sugar evenly over the frying bananas. Stir gently so the sugar melts and coats the bananas as it caramelizes. The sugar will stick to the bananas and form a sweet glaze.
  4. Cook until golden brown: Continue frying for about 5–7 minutes, occasionally flipping the bananas with tongs or a spatula to ensure even coating and cooking.
  5. Skewer the bananas: Remove the caramelized bananas from the pan and let them cool slightly. Skewer 2–3 bananas onto bamboo sticks for serving (optional).
  6. Serve and enjoy: Allow the banana cue to cool for a few minutes (the caramelized sugar will be very hot). Enjoy as a snack or dessert!
