Taho

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Silken tofu
  • 1/2 cup Sago pearls
  • 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sago pearls: Boil the sago pearls in water until translucent, about 15-20 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the syrup: In a saucepan, combine the brown sugar and water, and heat until the sugar dissolves and the syrup thickens. Add vanilla for extra flavor if desired.
  3. Prepare the tofu: Warm the silken tofu by steaming it for a few minutes, or microwave it gently.
  4. Assemble: In a glass or cup, layer the tofu, then add a spoonful of sago pearls, and top with brown sugar syrup.
  5. Serve: Stir and enjoy your sweet, comforting taho!
