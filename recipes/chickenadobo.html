Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces (about 3-4 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup Vinegar
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 3 Bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon Brown sugar (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 cup Water
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, combine the chicken with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaves, and peppercorns. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or overnight for more flavor.
  2. Simmer the chicken: In a pot, add the marinated chicken along with the marinade, water, and brown sugar (if using). Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for about 25-30 minutes, until the chicken is tender and fully cooked.
  3. Fry (optional): For extra flavor, heat some oil in a pan and fry the chicken pieces until they are golden brown and crispy.
  4. Final simmer: Return the fried chicken to the pot and simmer for an additional 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice.
