Halo-halo

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup Sweetened jackfruit (langka), cut into strips
  • 1/4 cup Sweetened saba bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Sweetened red mung beans (mungo)
  • 1/4 cup Sweetened macapuno (coconut sport), shredded
  • 1/4 cup Leche flan, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup Ube halaya (purple yam), mashed
  • 1/4 cup Pinipig (crispy rice flakes) (optional)
  • 1/4 cup Evaporated milk
  • 1 scoop Ube ice cream (optional)
  • Sugar (optional, depending on the sweetness of ingredients)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Make sure all the sweetened ingredients (jackfruit, saba bananas, mung beans, macapuno, etc.) are ready, and cut the leche flan and ube halaya into cubes or portions.
  2. Layer the ingredients: In a tall glass, start by adding a layer of sweetened ingredients, such as jackfruit, bananas, and mung beans. Follow with a layer of leche flan, ube halaya, and macapuno.
  3. Add ice: Fill the glass with shaved ice, about 3/4 full.
  4. Top it off: Add a scoop of ube ice cream on top, if desired. Sprinkle some pinipig for crunch.
  5. Pour milk: Drizzle evaporated milk over the ice and ingredients. Add sugar if you prefer more sweetness.
  6. Mix and enjoy: Mix everything together before eating to enjoy the colorful and delicious combination of flavors!
