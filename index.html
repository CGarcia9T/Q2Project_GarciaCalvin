Discover and share your favorite Filipino recipes!

Turon

Turon is a Filipino snack made by wrapping sweet saba banana (and sometimes jackfruit) in a spring roll wrapper, frying it, and coating it in caramelized sugar for a sweet, crunchy treat.

Kare-kare

Kare-Kare is a Filipino stew made with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables in a creamy peanut sauce, often served with shrimp paste on the side.

Halo-halo

Halo-Halo is a Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, mixed fruits, sweet beans, and toppings like ice cream and leche flan.
